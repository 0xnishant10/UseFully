“Usefully” is an online marketplace for university students where they can sell their old goods and possessions to other university students that they are no more in need of. For those students who do not want to sell but rarely use their possession, we are introducing a renting feature where they could rent their belongings to the students in their own university. For example, during an exam, if someone needs a calculator, they can look up on usefully if someone has listed a calculator for renting out and if there’s a calculator listed for renting, that person can rent that calculator out. Our aim is to create a user friendly platform that could open the doors of opportunities and side income for students.

Our project aims to solve the problem of irresponsible shopping and idleness, and on the other side creating opportunities for university students to help their pocket by doing the right thing. We are creating a sense of ownership and helping the needy at the right time by connecting them with the right person at the right time. We want to help every student and give them an opportunity to use their possessions correctly. We believe in converting Students liabilities into assets without any hassle and easy process. We want students to start using their possessions in the right way and should know how to make money out of thin air.
